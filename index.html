Metropolitan Planning Organization Map

This is an interactive map of Metropolitan Planning Organizations in the United States.

Here's what you can do with this map:

  • Click within an MPO's boundaries to identify the MPO and get details.
  • Search for MPOs by name.
  • Search for an address or place.
  • Zoom to your current location and identify the MPO there.
